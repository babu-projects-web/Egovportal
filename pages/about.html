About NagarSeva

Empowering citizens through digital governance

Our Mission

Building a Responsive City Government

NagarSeva is the official e-governance portal of the Municipal Corporation, designed to bridge the gap between citizens and civic authorities. Our mission is to make government services accessible, transparent, and responsive to every resident.

Through this platform, citizens can submit service requests, track resolution progress, and provide feedback — all without visiting a government office.
